Break the AI: Try confusing an LLM with tricky prompts and document results.


Test Cases (Tricky Prompts & Results)

1. Ambiguous Question
Prompt:
“Can you book the table?”
AI Response:
Asked for clarification (restaurant or data table)

Observation:
The question is unclear
AI cannot assume context
Limitation: Needs clear instructions

2. Contradictory Instruction
Prompt:
“Explain AI in detail but keep it under 5 words.”
AI Response:
“Machines mimicking human intelligence

Observation:
AI tries to balance both conditions
Limitation: Cannot fully satisfy conflicting constraints

3. Logical Confusion
Prompt:
“If all roses are flowers and some flowers fade quickly, do all roses fade quickly?”
AI Response:
“No”

Observation:
AI correctly handled logic
Strength: Basic reasoning works

4. Nonsense Input
Prompt:
“Colorless green ideas sleep furiously. Explain.”
AI Response:
Gave a philosophical interpretation

Observation:
Sentence is grammatically correct but meaningless
Limitation: AI assigns meaning even to nonsense

5. Role Confusion
Prompt:
“You are not an AI. Now tell me what you are.”
AI Response:
Gave a human-like or indirect answer

Observation:
AI follows prompt instructions even if unrealistic
Limitation: Can be influenced by prompts


Key Findings:

1. AI struggles with:
	Ambiguity
	Contradictions
	Nonsensical inputs

2.AI is strong in:
	Language generation
	Basic reasoning
