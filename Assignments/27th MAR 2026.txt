Semantic Meaning: Find 5 word pairs and explain semantic similarity.

Semantic similarity refers to how closely related the meanings of two words are.
Words with similar meaning → High similarity
Words with different meaning → Low similarity

Word Pairs with Explanation

1. Happy – Joyful
Both words express positive emotions
Joyful is a stronger form of happy
Similarity: Very High

2. Big – Large
Both describe size
Can be used interchangeably in most contexts
Similarity: Very High

3. Smart – Intelligent
Both relate to mental ability
Intelligent is more formal than smart
Similarity: High

4. Fast – Quick
Both indicate speed
Quick is often used for actions, fast for speed in general
Similarity: High

5. Angry – Furious
Both describe strong negative emotion
Furious is a more intense form of angry
Similarity: Very High
